Synopsis

In this groundbreaking exploration, acclaimed astrophysicist Dr. Elena Rostova peels back the layers of the observable universe to reveal the intricate geometric framework that dictates everything from planetary orbits to galactic superclusters.

Through accessible analogies and stunning conceptual visualizations, "The Architecture of the Cosmos" demystifies dark matter, gravitational waves, and the elusive string theory, presenting a unified vision of a highly structured cosmos.
